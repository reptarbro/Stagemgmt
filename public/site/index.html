What it is

One quiet home for the whole prompt book

StandBy is a digital prompt book. Everything a stage manager carries from show to show, in one place and cross-referenced — nine linked modules that replace a binder and a fistful of spreadsheets.

Production Hub People Schedule Scenes Props & Costumes Line Notes Script Cue-to-Cue Reports

The whole prompt book, in one place

Everything about a production lives together and cross-references. Calls know who's cast, availability conflicts flag against the schedule, scenes link to events — and as tech approaches, your calling script surfaces automatically with standbys, cues, and status.

Works in the room — offline & private

No account required and no wifi dependency. Your show is stored privately on the device, it loads instantly, and it keeps working when the venue wifi doesn't. One tap backs up the whole production as a file — and it's the same app on every screen.

Offline-first Private on device One-tap backup iPad · laptop · phone

Produces the paperwork you hand out

Print-perfect contact sheets, cast lists, and rehearsal reports; clean PDFs with a pre-filled email; photo-upload to file a physically-signed sheet against a past call; and a calendar export for the company's phones.

The case

Why StandBy, not five other tools

One tool shaped around the stage manager's workflow — instead of a spreadsheet, a shared calendar, report templates, paper sign-in sheets, and a physical binder.

Replaces a stack

Contacts spreadsheet, shared calendar, report templates, paper sign-in sheets, and the physical prompt book — collapsed into one tool.

Not a generic PM app

Asana, Notion, and Trello make you build a stage-management system. StandBy already is one — zero configuration, usable on day one.

Not a locked-in SaaS

No subscription and no forced login. Your contact sheet lives on your device — not seat-gated on someone else's server.

Everywhere, one link

Full sidebar app on a laptop, a touch layout on the iPad, a pocket reference on a phone. Nothing to install.

Built for the field

From Off-Off-Broadway through Equity

Small & self-produced

  • Free and installs nothing — fits companies with no budget and no IT.
  • Runs on your own iPad, with no account to provision — usable day one.
  • Export & import covers the "SM changes, the show goes on" handoff.
  • A sample production teaches the whole tool in one tap.

Union & professional

  • Distribution-ready reports and callboard sign-in sheets.
  • Attendance — present, late, absent, excused, no-show, with timing notes.
  • Emergency contacts and availability/conflict tracking that respects actors.
  • Language tuned to the practice, not generic project jargon.

Sign in once with Google or email and your whole production syncs from iPad to laptop to phone — automatically, in the background. Start blocking on the iPad in rehearsal, finish the report on your laptop at home.
